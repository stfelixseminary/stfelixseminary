Rev. Fr. Francis Ofozor

Uneze-Aham, Aniocha North

Parish Priest

Rev. Fr. Francis Ofozor

Born 1961 | Fondly known as Akpumwu

Pastoral Minister across Multiple Parishes

Hailing from Uneze-Aham in Aniocha North, Fr. Ofozor is known for his simplicity, zeal, and unwavering commitment to pastoral care. After completing his education at Uneze-Aham Community Primary School and training in building materials at Onitsha, he answered the call to priesthood at St. Felix Seminary and continued at SS. Peter & Paul Major Seminary, Ibadan. Over the years, he has served passionately in parishes across the Diocese including Ubuluku, Oko, Ekuoma, and Agbor.

Pastoral Dedication Award

Priest-in-Charge

Rev. Fr. Sylvester Achanyu

Ordained 2011

Priest-in-Charge at St. Joseph Catholic Church, NsuKwa

A dedicated shepherd with remarkable commitment to the Diocese. He began at St. John the Baptist Catholic Church, Agbor, and later returned to St. Felix Seminary as Dean of Students (2012-2018), shaping many young seminarians. He continued as Assistant Parish Priest at St. John Mary Vianney Asaba. A cheerful and dynamic priest, he remains committed to building faith and community everywhere he goes.
